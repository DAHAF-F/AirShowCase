HOW IT WORKS

Three things happen, in order

01

A camera watches the booth

Any laptop or kiosk webcam works. No depth sensor, no headset, no app to download. The visitor just walks up and raises a hand.

02

Hand tracking drives the product

21 points per hand are tracked in real time. Palm position sets where your product floats; hand rotation and spread set its angle and scale.

03

A pinch locks the moment

Pinching fingers together locks a colourway or variant — the natural "yes, this one" gesture — and can trigger a lead-capture screen or a printed receipt at the booth.
